Mountain Biking

Mountain biking is a sport that involves riding a bike on offroad trails. The trails can vary from something as basic as a flat dirt path to a steep, narrow trail covered with rocks and roots.

2 Types of Bikes

There are 2 main bike designs: full suspension or hardtail. A hardtail has suspension for the front wheel only while a full suspension bike has suspension elements for the front and rear wheels.

  1. Hardtails
  2. Full Suspension

Disciplines

There are several different disciplines in the sport. The 3 biggest are cross country, all mountain, and downhill.

Cross Country All Mountain Downhill
Cross country (or XC) bikes are designed for maximum efficency on flat ground and climbs. They can be full suspension or hardtails. Suspension travel ranges from 80-110mm.
